Current Projects

Research questions I’m actively working on

These summaries are short and web-friendly, so they can evolve as papers and collaborations move forward.

Labor and climate

Extreme temperature, work hours, and heat standards

This project studies how very high temperatures affect labor supply in the United States and whether workplace heat standards reduce those effects.

Health and environment

Drought exposure and birth outcomes in the Philippines

Using household survey data linked with gridded drought measures, this work examines how prenatal drought exposure shapes prematurity and birth weight outcomes.

Education and labor markets

Returns to non-cognitive skills training

A meta-analysis of global evidence on how non-cognitive skills affect earnings and how training programs can build those skills over time.
